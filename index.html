About Us

Bridging Ancient Wisdom & Modern Science

At Serene Path Wellness, we believe true healing happens when we address the whole person – body, mind, and spirit. Our team of certified practitioners brings together the timeless wisdom of Ayurveda, meditation, and spiritual traditions with evidence-based psychological approaches.
